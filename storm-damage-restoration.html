Immediate Response Protocol

West Virginia storms can be brutal. From heavy mountain snow to intense summer hail, your roof is the first line of defense. When that defense is compromised, seconds count.

Emergency Tarping

Rapid deployment to stop leaks immediately and prevent further water damage to your home's interior. We arrive within hours, not days.

Damage Documentation

Comprehensive photo documentation and detailed reports for your insurance claim. We work directly with your adjuster to expedite the process.

Structural Assessment

Expert evaluation of roof integrity, decking condition, and potential safety hazards. You'll know exactly what needs to be done.

Complete Restoration

From emergency repairs to full roof replacement, we restore your roof to better-than-original condition using premium materials.

Specialized Storm Damage Repair

Every type of storm damage requires specialized expertise. Our crews are trained to identify and repair damage that others might miss.

Hail Damage

Bruised shingles, dented metal surfaces, and compromised granule protection often hidden from ground level. Our trained inspectors find what others miss, including subtle damage that will lead to premature failure. We document every impact for your insurance claim and restore full protection.

Wind Damage

Missing shingles, lifted metal panels, broken seals, and impact damage from flying debris. Wind damage compromises your roof's weather barrier and can lead to catastrophic leaks. We provide complete restoration of your roof's protective seal and structural integrity.

Ice & Snow Damage

Weight-related stress, ice damming, gutter damage, and freeze-thaw cycles that crack and split roofing materials. West Virginia winters are harsh—our team specializes in winter weather solutions including proper ventilation and ice prevention systems.

Lightning & Fire

Direct lightning strikes and subsequent fire damage require immediate attention and specialized restoration techniques. We assess electrical system impacts, structural damage, and provide comprehensive rebuilding services with enhanced lightning protection.

Tree & Debris Impact

Fallen trees, heavy branches, and storm debris can cause catastrophic structural damage. We handle emergency debris removal, tarp installation, structural reinforcement, and complete roof reconstruction with priority scheduling.

Water Intrusion

Storm-driven rain can find its way through the smallest openings, causing extensive interior damage. We locate all entry points, repair roof penetrations, replace saturated materials, and prevent mold growth with rapid response.
