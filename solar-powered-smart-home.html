Solar Financing & Incentives

Make solar affordable with flexible financing and maximize your savings with federal, state, and local incentives.

Federal Incentives

Take advantage of substantial federal benefits that make solar more affordable than ever before.

  • 30% Federal Investment Tax Credit (ITC)
  • Depreciation deductions for commercial systems
  • Modified Accelerated Cost-Recovery System (MACRS)
  • Reduced installation costs through incentives

State & Local Programs

West Virginia offers additional incentives and programs to support your transition to solar energy.

  • West Virginia solar rebates and incentives
  • Property tax exemptions for solar installations
  • Local utility solar programs and net metering
  • Performance-based incentives
